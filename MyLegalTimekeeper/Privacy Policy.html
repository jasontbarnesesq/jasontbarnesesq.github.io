Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

Welcome to My Legal Timekeeper ("App," "we," "our," or "us"). Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to operate the App.

Information you provide directly

  • Time entries
  • Notes or descriptions you enter
  • Optional account information (name or email if used for login)

Information collected automatically

  • Basic technical data (browser type, device type, timestamps)
  • Anonymous usage analytics (if enabled)
  • Google account information (if you connect Google services)

If you authorize Google sign-in or Google Drive integration, we may access:

  • Your email address (for authentication only)
  • Files you explicitly choose to create or access through the App

We do not access any other Google data.

2. How We Use Information

We use information solely to:

  • Provide timekeeping functionality
  • Save and sync your data
  • Export or store files you request
  • Improve reliability and performance
  • Provide support when requested

We do not sell or rent your personal data.

3. Data Storage

  • Data is stored locally in your browser and/or in your connected Google services
  • We do not maintain independent marketing databases
  • We retain data only as long as necessary for app functionality

4. Sharing of Information

We do not share personal data except:

  • With service providers necessary to operate the app (e.g., hosting)
  • When required by law
  • With your explicit consent

5. Your Rights

You may:

  • Delete your data at any time
  • Disconnect Google permissions
  • Request deletion of any stored information
  • Stop using the app at any time

6. Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards. However, no system is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for children under 13.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. Updates will be posted here with a new date.
